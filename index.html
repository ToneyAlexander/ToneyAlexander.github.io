About Me

My name is Alex Toney, and I’m currently an 2nd year honors student at The Ohio State University, majoring in Computer Science & Engineering and minoring in Linguistics. I have been interested in computers for many years, and I first started programming in middle school. Last year, I was on a team that competed in the Fundamentals of Engineering Honors robot competition and placed third in the head-to-head competition. Recently I have become interested in artificial intelligence and have been reading about the topic. At some point I would like to look into natural language processing, but for now am only focusing on the general topic of AI.
This site will mainly act as a place to display interesting things I have programmed when they are compatible with a web format. I will also be using this site to share things I have learned, as well as other accomplishments related to my academic interests.
